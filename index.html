Fast and Accurate Species Tree Estimation using Consensus Methods


Supervised by:Dr. Md. Shamsuzzoha Bayzid (Professor, CSE, BUET)

Supervised by: Dr. Rezwana Reaz (Assistant Professor, CSE, BUET)

Status: Manuscript in progress

A species tree is a branching diagram or a tree showing the evolutionary relationships among various biological species. This study developed a two third consensus method which promise to keep the false positive rate zero in species tree estimation.
